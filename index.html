Hello, I'm Audrey!

I am a final-year PhD student in Computer Science at UC Berkeley in the Sky Computing Lab, advised by Ion Stoica and Natacha Crooks. My research focuses on transaction processing for database systems, particularly on leveraging scheduling algorithms to improve performance. I also work closely with the Themis team at Meta, focusing on consistency and isolation in large-scale systems. Previously, I graduated with a BSE from Princeton University, where I worked with Wyatt Lloyd and Kai Li. I am supported by an NSF Graduate Research Fellowship, a Meta PhD Research Fellowship, and a Berkeley Chancellor's Fellowship.
